# **App Name**: OM Saravana Billing

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Provides a summary of all key data points across purchases, sales, products, and transactions for a quick business health check.
- Purchase Management: Allows adding, viewing, and editing supplier details, credit, and payment information.
- Sales Management: Enables managing customer details, credit, and payments with functionalities to add, view, and edit sales entries.
- Product Catalog: Manages the product inventory with fields for item code, name, and multiple pricing rates.
- Transaction Reporting: Generates reports on sales and purchase entries filtered by customer and date, aiding in financial analysis and reconciliation.
- Settings & Profile Management: Allows the shop owner to manage their profile details and securely logout of the application.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#228B22) to align with the fresh vegetable theme.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) to provide a clean and spacious feel, as requested by the user.
- Accent color: Light green (#90EE90) for highlighting interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a modern, neutral sans-serif suitable for both headings and body text.
- Left sidebar navigation for main menus: Dashboard, Purchase, Sales, Products, Transaction Report, Credits, and Settings.
- Use of data tables for displaying lists of purchase, sales, and product entries with editable fields.
